    So, let's start with what are road accidents. I guess it is needless to say this as I assume most of you have already witnessed them or experienced them, but, it is my duty to say about it to those who don't know about it. A road traffic accident (RTA) is any injury due to crashes originating from, terminating with or involving a vehicle partially or fully on a public road. Now, that you know what a road accident is, let's see how does it happen. Nowadays, people are too busy to even lift their heads up and all of them are after time! Nobody seems to have even 1 minute to spare and due to this they always in a hurry, which mainly causes accidents. They don't have time cause they don't know how to manage time. For such people, all I would like to say is, first learn to manage time and then get on to the road, if you can't, its better for you to work from home or use public transport. There are many other causes of accidents too. In some areas, there is no traffic police and no traffic controls. In recent years, a lot has improved and the government has taken many steps to ensure that there is no hindrance in controlling traffic from their side. I guess you have understood how and why accidents happen. That said, let's see some things about carbon emissions (CO2).

If not electric, at least shift from BS4 to BS6 variants. I know its hard, but we need to do it. But the poor who cannot afford it, wont be able to do anything about it and on that part, we fail. To succeed in this part itself, more attention should be paid to human resource development, so that people can get more educated, earn more money and contribute to the nation that uplifted them and gave them a chance to live with their heads up.
